# Email Templates for Scout Drafting

Templates use variables like `%NAME%`, `%CONTENT_TOPIC%`, etc. that get replaced during generation.

## Template: mom-influencer

**Use for:** Parenting/lifestyle influencers with kids aged 4-12

```
Subject: Quick question about %CONTENT_TOPIC%

Hi %NAME%,

I just saw your post about %CONTENT_TOPIC% — %CONTENT_HOOK_REF%

I'm Keri from Thoven, a music education platform designed for busy parents like you. We've built something that helps kids learn music without the scheduling nightmares or expensive private lessons.

%PERSONALIZATION_BLOCK%

Would you be open to a quick chat about how this might resonate with your audience? No pitch, just curious if it solves a problem you're already hearing about.

Best,
Keri
Thoven | keri@thoven.co
```

**Why this works:**
- Opens with genuine content reference (shows we follow them)
- Clear positioning (busy parents, no scheduling nightmares)
- Soft CTA ("quick chat," "no pitch")
- Keri's signature (human, approachable)

## Template Variables Reference

| Variable | Description | Example |
|:---------|:------------|:--------|
| `%NAME%` | First name only | "Sarah" |
| `%FULL_NAME%` | Full name | "Sarah Johnson" |
| `%CONTENT_TOPIC%` | Topic of recent post | "homeschool routines" |
| `%CONTENT_HOOK_REF%` | Hook/intro from their content | "the way you described morning chaos..." |
| `%PERSONALIZATION_BLOCK%` | Auto-generated paragraph | Dynamic based on research |
| `%CITY%` | City from bio | "Austin" |
| `%LOCATION%` | Full location | "Austin, TX" |
| `%PLATFORM%` | Social platform | "Instagram" |
| `%HANDLE%` | Social handle | "@sarahhschools" |

## Subject Line Guidelines

**Do:**
- Reference specific content ("About your post on...")
- Ask genuine questions
- Keep under 50 characters if possible

**Don't:**
- Use clickbait ("You won't believe...")
- All caps or excessive punctuation
- Generic partnership spam ("Partnership opportunity!!!")

**Good examples:**
- "Quick question about your homeschool routine"
- "Music curriculum that actually works"
- "Following your journey in Austin"

---

## Email Length Guidelines

| Section | Target Length |
|:--------|:--------------|
| Opening | 1-2 sentences |
| Intro/Value prop | 1-2 sentences |
| Personalization | 2-3 sentences |
| CTA | 1 sentence |
| **Total** | **100-200 words** |

Shorter is better. Busy people skim.
