# Scoring Rubric

Thoven prospect scoring system (1-5 scale) with specific criteria for each tier.

## Overall Score Formula

| Component | Weight | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Followers | 25% | Audience size and reach potential |
| Engagement | 30% | Likes, comments, saves relative to followers |
| Content Fit | 25% | Alignment with music education/homeschool |
| Has Website | 10% | Blog/website presence (professionalism) |
| Activity | 10% | Posting frequency (last 30 days) |

## Tier Definitions

### 🌟 Score 5 (S-Tier) — PRIORITY

**Criteria:**
- **Followers:** 200K+ OR highly engaged micro (50K+ with 5%+ engagement)
- **Engagement:** 5%+ (exceptional)
- **Content Fit:** High alignment — homeschool + music/arts education content
- **Website:** Has professional blog or website
- **Activity:** Active within 7 days

**Action:** Personalized outreach within 24 hours

**Example:** Homeschool mom with 150K followers, posts weekly music curriculum reviews, 6% engagement, runs a homeschool blog.

---

### ⭐ Score 4 (A-Tier) — HIGH

**Criteria:**
- **Followers:** 50K-200K
- **Engagement:** 3-5% (above average)
- **Content Fit:** Strong — homeschool OR parenting with education focus
- **Website:** Has blog or link-in-bio presence
- **Activity:** Active within 14 days

**Action:** Personalized outreach, mention specific content

**Example:** Mom blogger with 75K followers, posts about kids' activities, 4% engagement, has parenting blog.

---

### 🟠 Score 3 (B-Tier) — MEDIUM

**Criteria:**
- **Followers:** 10K-50K
- **Engagement:** 1.5-3% (average)
- **Content Fit:** Moderate — parenting/lifestyle with potential for music overlap
- **Website:** May or may not have website
- **Activity:** Active within 30 days

**Action:** Templated outreach, worth testing

**Example:** Local mom influencer with 25K followers, posts family content, 2% engagement.

---

### 🟡 Score 2 (C-Tier) — LOW

**Criteria:**
- **Followers:** 1K-10K
- **Engagement:** 0.5-1.5% (below average)
- **Content Fit:** Weak — lifestyle without clear education angle
- **Website:** No website
- **Activity:** Sporadic (30-90 days)

**Action:** Deprioritize, batch outreach later

**Example:** Small account with 5K followers, inconsistent posting, generic content.

---

### ⚫ Score 1 (D-Tier) — SKIP

**Criteria:**
- **Followers:** <1K
- **Engagement:** <0.5%
- **Content Fit:** None — misaligned audience or content
- **Website:** No presence
- **Activity:** Inactive 90+ days

**Action:** Do not contact

**Red Flags:**
- Bot/inauthentic followers
- Inactive accounts
- Adult-only content
- Controversial/political focus
- No kids/mom content

---

## Component Scoring Details

### Follower Count Scoring

| Followers | Score | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| 200K+ | 5 | Major reach, broader audience |
| 50K-200K | 4 | Strong reach, still relatable |
| 10K-50K | 3 | Sweet spot for micro-influencer |
| 1K-10K | 2 | Growing, limited reach |
| <1K | 1 | Too small for partnership |

### Engagement Rate Scoring

Engagement = (Likes + Comments) / Followers × 100

| Engagement | Score | Notes |
|------------|-------|-------|
| 5%+ | 5 | Exceptional, highly engaged |
| 3-5% | 4 | Above average, strong |
| 1.5-3% | 3 | Average for most niches |
| 0.5-1.5% | 2 | Below average |
| <0.5% | 1 | Poor, likely bots |

**Benchmarks by platform:**
- Instagram: 1-3% is average, 5%+ is excellent
- TikTok: 3-8% is average, 10%+ is excellent

### Content Fit Scoring

| Level | Score | Characteristics |
|-------|-------|-----------------|
| **High** | 5 | Posts about: homeschool, music education, kids activities, educational toys, piano lessons |
| **Medium** | 3 | Posts about: parenting, mom life, family activities, lifestyle with kids |
| **Low** | 1 | Posts about: fashion, beauty, travel without kids, adult-only content |

### Website Presence

| Has Website | Score | Notes |
|-------------|-------|-------|
| Yes (active blog) | 5 | Professional, content depth |
| Yes (basic/parking) | 3 | Some effort, minimal content |
| No (linktree only) | 2 | Acceptable for micro |
| No (nothing) | 1 | Hard to validate |

### Activity Score

| Last Post | Score | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Within 7 days | 5 | Very active |
| Within 14 days | 4 | Active |
| Within 30 days | 3 | Moderate |
| 30-90 days | 2 | Sporadic |
| 90+ days | 1 | Inactive |

## Auto-Reject Criteria

Skip immediately if any of these:

- 🚫 Engagement <0.5% (bot indicators)
- 🚫 No contact info AND no website
- 🚫 Inactive 90+ days
- 🚫 Adult-only content (no kids/family)
- 🚫 Controversial focus (politics, divisive topics)
- 🚫 Location outside target markets (initial: US only)
- 🚫 Private account
- 🚫 Under 1,000 followers (unless hyper-local with high engagement)
