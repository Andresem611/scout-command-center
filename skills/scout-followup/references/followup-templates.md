# Follow-Up Message Templates

Reference templates for Thoven prospect follow-up cadence.

---

## Day 3: Gentle Bump

### Purpose
Get a response without being pushy. Assume they're busy, not uninterested.

### Characteristics
- **Tone:** Light, assumptive, brief
- **Length:** 2-3 sentences
- **Goal:** Re-surface the message

### Template

```
Hi {First Name},

Just bumping this to the top of your inbox — know things get busy on the studio side.

Worth a 10-minute chat to see if Thoven could bring you a few extra students per month?

Best,
Andres
```

### Variations

**Short version (LinkedIn):**
```
Hi {First Name}, bumping this up — know you’re busy. Worth 10 mins to explore if Thoven could send a few students your way?
```

**With specific hook:**
```
Hi {First Name},

Quick follow-up — noticed you just posted about expanding your {instrument} program. Thoven might help fill those new slots faster.

Still worth a brief chat?
```

## Day 7: Value-Add (Different Angle)

### Purpose
Provide a new insight or angle they haven't heard yet. Show you've thought about their specific situation.

### Characteristics
- **Tone:** Helpful, insightful, relevant
- **Length:** 3-4 sentences
- **Goal:** Add value, not just ask for time

### Template

```
Hi {First Name},

Quick thought: instead of competing with online lesson platforms, Thoven partners with them to fill your local slots.

We're already working with {Example School} in {City} — they've seen {Result} since joining.

Worth a brief call to explore if it fits {Studio Name}?

Best,
Andres
```

### Angle Options

| Angle | Best For | Hook |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Competition → Partnership** | Studios worried about online platforms | Position Thoven as partner, not competitor |
| **Student Quality** | Studios tired of uncommitted students | Emphasize pre-qualified, motivated students |
| **Zero Risk** | Risk-averse owners | Free to join, only pay when students enroll |
| **Local Focus** | Community-centered studios | Highlight local student matching |
| **Seasonal** | Before school year / January | Timing: "Before the enrollment rush" |

### Variation Examples

**Student Quality Angle:**
```
Hi {First Name},

Different angle: Thoven pre-qualifies students for commitment level before matching. Most studios tell us their Thoven students practice more and stay longer.

Worth seeing if the fit is right for {Studio Name}?
```

**Zero Risk Angle:**
```
Hi {First Name},

Realized I didn't mention: Thoven is free to join. You only pay when a matched student actually enrolls.

No upfront cost, no monthly fees — just new students when we make a match.

Still worth a 10-minute call to see how it works?
```

## Day 14: Soft Close / Final Attempt

### Purpose
Get a clear yes/no. Respect their time, but be direct about closing the loop.

### Characteristics
- **Tone:** Direct, respectful, decisive
- **Length:** 3-4 sentences
- **Goal:** Final response or clear close

### Template

```
Hi {First Name},

I'll be direct: I've reached out a couple times and don't want to clutter your inbox if now's not the right time.

If Thoven's student-matching model isn't a priority for {Studio Name} currently, totally get it — just say the word and I'll close the loop.

If it *is* interesting, I'm here: {Calendar Link}

Best,
Andres
```

### Variations

**Polite archive:**
```
Hi {First Name},

Don't want to keep following up if the timing's off. Should I check back in a few months, or close this out for now?

Either way, best of luck with {Studio Name}.
```

**Final value push:**
```
Hi {First Name},

Last touch — we're onboarding 3 new studios in {City} this month and I wanted to make sure {Studio Name} had a shot if you're interested.

If not, no worries. Just reply "not now" and I'll close the loop.

{Calendar Link}
```

## Response Handling

| Response | Action | Next Step |
|:---|:---|:---|
| "Not interested" | Update stage to `Declined` | Archive with note |
| "Not now, check back in X" | Set reminder for X | Keep in `Contacted` |
| "Tell me more" | Update stage to `Responded` | Send details, schedule call |
| "Send pricing" | Update stage to `Responded` | Send pricing PDF |
| No response after Day 14 | Update stage to `Declined` | Archive, review in 3 months |

---

## Tone Guidelines

| Do | Don't |
|:---|:---|
| Assume they're busy | Assume they're not interested |
| Offer value in each touch | Just ask "did you get my email?" |
| Be specific to their studio | Use generic templates without customization |
| Give them an easy out | Pressure or guilt-trip |
| Sign with your name only | Use "Best regards from the Thoven Team" |
